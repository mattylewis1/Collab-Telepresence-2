Collaborative Telepresence

Welcome to my page, here I will inform you on what Collaborative Telepresence is and how it can effect how we live .

Definition

Technology-based process of communicating using electronic assets and accompanying software which allow a person to feel as if they were at a place other than their true location. Components such as mobile devices and virtual reality headsets which combine the use of video, audio and on-screen drawing capabilities using modern technology to enable multi-party conferencing in real-time, independent of location.

My personal definition of collaborative telepresence is the use of any modern device that can utilize digital video and networking technology to enable remote individuals to interact as if they were in the same room.

Collaborative Telepresence has been changing the way we interact with people and our environment for the past decade and continues to expand possibiles with newer techlogies being developed each day. We will be identifing a few area where collaborative telepresence has had a huge impact.

  • Impact on Healthcare
  • Impact on Education
